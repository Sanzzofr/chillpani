About us

AirDoot is a revolutionary online platform built with the purpose of solving complex hands-on service problems for all industries such as HVAC, Electric, Plumbing, Fire and Security, using technology as a primary tool. Whether it is a residential or commercial space, AirDoot's online platform will cater to every type of end-user. Launched in 2021, AirDoot is currently catering the HVAC&R services and is rapidly scaling up its operation across India & Singapore.

Van - Man - Can!

The platform aims to micromanage and efficiently deliver all services by bringing every stakeholder such as customer, service provider, part supplier, and other ancillary service providers under one roof. The platform will allow customers to book services and efficiently track their services using the AirDoot web and mobile app. Currently, a customer can book services for Air Conditioning, Refrigeration, Ventilation & Ancillary products. All services listed on AirDoot are delivered by trained professionals and partners.
